INSTIL

/IN'STIL/

verb: instill
1. gradually but firmly establish (an idea or attitude) in a person's mind.
"the standards her parents had instilled into her"
synonyms: inculcate, implant, fix, ingrain, infuse, impress, imprint, introduce;
2. put (a substance) into something in the form of liquid drops.
"she was told how to instil eye drops"
synonyms: administer, introduce, add gradually, infuse, inject
"she was told how to instil eye drops".
